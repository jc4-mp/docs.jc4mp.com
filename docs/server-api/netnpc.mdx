#### `NetNPC:Move(direction: vec3, moveType: MoveType, moveStyle: MoveStyle): void`

Specifies in which direction the NPC should move. The NPC will keep moving until an idle movement type is specified.

**Parameters:**
- `direction: vec3` - The direction to move in
- `moveType: MoveType` - The type of movement (Walk, Run, Sprint, Idle)
- `moveStyle: MoveStyle` - The style of movement (Normal, Search, etc.)

**Example:**
```lua
-- Make NPC walk forward
npc:Move(vec3(1, 0, 0), MoveType.Walk, MoveStyle.Normal)

-- Make NPC sprint to the right
npc:Move(vec3(0, 0, 1), MoveType.Sprint, MoveStyle.Patrol)

-- Make NPC idle
npc:Move(vec3(0, 0, 0), MoveType.Idle, MoveStyle.Normal)
```

#### `NetNPC:LookAt(position: vec3, type: LookType): void`

Makes the NPC look at a specific position.

**Parameters:**
- `position: vec3` - The position to look at
- `type: LookType` - How the NPC should look (Head or Body)

**Example:**
```lua
-- Make NPC look at position with just the head
npc:LookAt(vec3(100, 50, 200), LookType.Head)

-- Make NPC turn their body to look at position
npc:LookAt(vec3(200, 100, 300), LookType.Body)
```

#### `NetNPC:SetHealth(health: int): void`

Sets the health of the NPC.

**Parameters:**
- `health: int` - The health value to set

**Example:**
```lua
npc:SetHealth(100)
```

#### `NetNPC:Destroy(): void`

Destroys the NPC.

**Example:**
```lua
npc:Destroy()
```

#### `NetNPC:Follow(objectToFollow: NetObject, moveType: MoveType, moveStyle: MoveStyle): void`

Makes the NPC follow a specified NetObject. The NPC will continuously move towards the target object.

**Parameters:**
- `objectToFollow: NetObject` - The object for the NPC to follow
- `moveType: MoveType` - The type of movement (Walk, Run, Sprint, Idle)
- `moveStyle: MoveStyle` - The style of movement (Normal, Search, etc.)

**Example:**
```lua
-- Make NPC follow a player
npc:Follow(player, MoveType.Run, MoveStyle.Normal)

-- Make NPC follow a vehicle
npc:Follow(vehicle, MoveType.Sprint, MoveStyle.Patrol)
```
